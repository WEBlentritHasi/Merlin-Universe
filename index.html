The show reimagines the legendary tale of Merlin, a young warlock, who must secretly use his magic to protect Prince Arthur as he rises to become the future King of Camelot.

MERLIN

Merlin is a young, powerful sorcerer in the BBC series Merlin, destined to protect Prince Arthur and help him become the future king of Camelot. Loyal, brave, and clever, Merlin's friendship with Arthur forms the heart of the show as he secretly guides him toward his legendary destiny.
